What is the purpose of this application?

This application helps farmers improve soil health and crop yields by providing data-driven fertilizer recommendations based on soil health, crop type, and weather conditions.

How does the application work?

The app analyzes data from soil tests, the type of crops planted, and local weather patterns. It then suggests the best type and amount of fertilizer to use for sustainable and productive farming.

What information do I need to provide?

• You’ll need to input the following: 1. Soil test results (NPK levels, pH, etc.) 2. The type of crop you're growing 3. Your location (for weather data)

How does this app help improve soil health?

By recommending the right amount of fertilizer, the app prevents overuse, which can lead to soil degradation. It ensures nutrients are added to the soil in a balanced and sustainable way.

Can the website handle different types of crops?

Yes, the website is designed to provide recommendations for various crop types. You just need to select the crop you are planting, and the app will adjust its recommendations accordingly.

How is the weather data used in recommendations?

Weather data, such as rainfall, temperature, and humidity, impacts how fertilizers are absorbed by plants and the soil. The app uses this data to fine-tune recommendations, ensuring that fertilizers are applied under the best conditions.
